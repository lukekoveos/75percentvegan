## Why This Works

Breakfast is the easiest meal to make plant-based. This burrito is filling, protein-rich, and takes 5 minutes.

## Ingredients

- 1 large tortilla
- 1/2 cup black beans (canned is fine)
- 2 scrambled eggs (or tofu scramble for fully vegan)
- Handful of spinach
- Salsa
- Hot sauce (optional but recommended)

## Instructions

1. Warm tortilla in a pan
2. Heat black beans (microwave or pan)
3. Scramble eggs/tofu
4. Layer everything on tortilla: beans, eggs, spinach, salsa
5. Roll it up
6. Done

**Total time:** 5 minutes  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Cost:** ~$2 per burrito

## Variations

- Add avocado if you're fancy
- Swap spinach for any greens
- Add roasted sweet potato cubes
- Use leftover rice for extra bulk
